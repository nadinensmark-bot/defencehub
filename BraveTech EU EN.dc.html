BraveTech EU
DefTech Forges

A joint initiative of the European Commission and Ukraine for defence innovation. Connects the European Defence Fund (EDF), EUDIS and the Ukrainian BRAVE1 platform — from DefTech Forge competitions through testing campaigns to follow-on EU funding instruments.

[ Phase 1 ]

DefTech Forges

Goal: filter the most promising solutions addressing real operational needs.
✔Weekly events held concurrently at 2 locations (twice per year in 2026, three times per year in 2027 and 2028).
✔First DefTech Forges: 1–5 June in France and 3–7 June in Estonia.
✔Brings together EU and Ukrainian innovators with solutions to operational challenges identified by Ukraine on the battlefield.
✔Startups, SMEs, individual innovators and large industry are all welcome.
✔Top 3 teams at each location receive €120,000 to further develop their solution and advance to Phase 2.
Operational challenges — Estonia 🇪🇪

Offensive and resilient unmanned aerial systems for contested environments.

Operational challenges — France 🇫🇷
  • Ground capabilities for force protection
  • Perimeter security
  • Close combat support in contested environments

EU Funding Tools

Phase 2 winners gain access to EU defence funding instruments for further development and deployment.

EUDIS

EU Defence Innovation Scheme — early-stage support up to €2M per innovator.

EDF

European Defence Fund — largest EU programme financing collaborative R&D in defence technologies.

BRAVE1

Ukrainian defence innovation platform for dual-use technologies with real battlefield validation.

[ Partnership ]

UNITE – Brave NATO

A partnership connecting BRAVE1 (Ukraine's defence innovation platform) with NATO DIANA to accelerate dual-use technologies tested on real battlefields into NATO standards.
