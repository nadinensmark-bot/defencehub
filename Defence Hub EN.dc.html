We connect startups, research, industry and investors to accelerate the creation and deployment of technologies for defence, security and resilience. We guide you to programs such as NATO DIANA, EUDIS, EDF and EIC — and open the door to the international ecosystem.

Our mission

We build the ecosystem

We build the Czech defence and dual-use ecosystem and connect its players — startups, industry, research, investors and the armed forces.

Advisory for SMEs & startups

We navigate companies through the defence world — from the defence use-case through programs to application preparation.

Communication with end users

We open a dialogue between innovators, the military and end users, so technology solves real needs.

BraveTech EUEU × UKRAINE

A joint European Commission–Ukraine initiative for defence innovation. Brings together the European Defence Fund (EDF), EUDIS and Ukraine's BRAVE1 platform.

  • ✔Up to €50M from the EU, matched by €50M from Ukraine
  • ✔Two phases: Seed and Scale Up
  • ✔EU–Ukraine matchmaking, joint development and testing
